Combining Consonants and Vowels
All consonants are connected to the vowels in the same way, called "Contracted Vowels"
Click on "Consonant Base" to choose a consonant, and then see how the vowels are added to it.
Click on "Equations" to see the elements of the compounds.
When you choose "Vowels", You can challenge yourself by pressing the keys on your keyboard to view individual letters.
The order goes in the following sequence: 123456789qwertyuiopasdfghjklzxcvbnm
Pressing the spacebar displays transliteration when viewing one letter.

Note the only two exceptions to the contracted letters: ru रु and rū रू.
